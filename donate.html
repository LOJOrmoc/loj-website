Support Our Mission

Every gift, no matter the size, helps us bring hope, worship, and community to more people in Ormoc City.

Why Your Support Matters

The Feast Ormoc is a free weekly gathering — we never charge for admission and we never turn anyone away. This is made possible entirely by the generosity of people who believe in this mission.

When you give to The Feast Ormoc, you are investing in lives changed by faith, families strengthened by community, and a city touched by the love of God. Your generosity makes it all possible.

Ways to Give

Choose the giving method that works best for you. Every gift makes an eternal difference.

📱

GCash

Give conveniently and securely through GCash. Contact us to receive the GCash account number and reference details for your donation.

admin@lojfamorm.online

Email us to request GCash donation details. Please include your name and "GCash Donation" in the subject line.

🏦

BPI Bank Transfer

Transfer your donation directly to our BPI bank account. Contact us to receive the account name, account number, and transfer instructions.

admin@lojfamorm.online

Email us to request BPI account details. Please send a screenshot of your transfer confirmation for our records.

🤲

Give at The Feast

The simplest and most personal way to give — place your offering during our weekly Sunday Feast gathering. A love offering is received during every service.

Every Sunday · 10:00 AM

Cinema 4, Robinsons Place Ormoc. No envelope needed — just come with a generous heart.
