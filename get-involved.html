Your Journey Starts Here

Get Involved

There is a place for you in this community. Choose your pathway and take the next step toward something meaningful.

Getting involved at The Feast Ormoc is simple — there are no complicated sign-up processes or requirements. We meet you where you are. Whether you want to simply attend, go deeper through a ministry, or fully commit to the LOJ community, every step is a step toward something greater.

🙏

Attend The Feast

The easiest and best first step is to simply come. No registration. No pressure. Just show up on a Sunday morning and experience The Feast for yourself.

  • ✓ Every Sunday at 10:00 AM
  • ✓ Cinema 4, Robinsons Place Ormoc
  • ✓ Free admission — always
  • ✓ No registration or appointment needed
  • ✓ Come alone or bring a friend
  • ✓ All are welcome, no exceptions
Plan Your Visit

Join a Ministry

Ready to go deeper? Our ministries are communities within the community — smaller, more personal groups where you grow in faith alongside people who share your season of life.

  • ✓ Youth Ministry — for young people
  • ✓ Singles Ministry — for single adults
  • ✓ Couples Ministry — for married couples
  • ✓ Small group gatherings and fellowship
  • ✓ Faith formation and discipleship
  • ✓ Contact us to find the right fit for you
Contact Us to Join

Become an LOJ Member

For those who feel called to go all in — becoming a member of the LOJ community means committing to a life of discipleship, service, and genuine fellowship with your brothers and sisters in Christ.

  • ✓ Join a small group (Familia)
  • ✓ Serve in a ministry or as a volunteer
  • ✓ Participate in formation programs
  • ✓ Serve the Ormoc community through outreach
  • ✓ Grow as a leader in faith and service
  • ✓ Be part of a nationwide movement
Reach Out to Us

Why Get Involved?

Community is not just a nice addition to faith — it is essential to it.

💛

Real Belonging

In a world where loneliness is common, The Feast Ormoc offers genuine community — people who know your name, celebrate your joys, and walk with you through difficulties.

📈

Spiritual Growth

Our ministries and small groups provide structure for real spiritual growth — not just inspiration once a week, but ongoing formation that transforms your daily life.

🎯

Purpose & Impact

When you serve — whether leading a ministry, volunteering at The Feast, or participating in outreach — you discover the deep satisfaction of living for something greater than yourself.

🌍

Community Service

As The Feast Ormoc grows, so does our capacity to serve Ormoc City. Getting involved means becoming part of the mission to bring love and hope to our neighbors.

Your First Step Is the Most Important

You do not need to have it all figured out. Just take one step — show up on Sunday, or reach out to us. We will walk the rest of the way together.
