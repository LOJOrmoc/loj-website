Who We Are

The Feast Ormoc is a vibrant Catholic charismatic community in Ormoc City, Leyte, Philippines. We are part of the Light of Jesus (LOJ) Family — a Spirit-filled nationwide movement founded by Bo Sanchez that brings the love of God to every Filipino through powerful worship, life-changing talks, and authentic community.

Every Sunday, hearts gather at Cinema 4, Robinsons Place Ormoc to encounter God through praise and worship, inspiring talks, and warm fellowship. Whether you are a lifelong Catholic, a curious seeker, or someone who simply needs hope — you belong here.

Our Vision

We dream of a community where every person in Ormoc City encounters the living God — where lives are transformed, families are healed, and hearts are set on fire with love and faith.

The Feast Ormoc exists to bring the love of Jesus to every corner of Ormoc City — one Sunday at a time, one life at a time. We believe that when people gather in His name, miracles happen.
