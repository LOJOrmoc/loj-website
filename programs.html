Faith in Action

Programs & Ministries

Discover how The Feast Ormoc serves God and community through worship, discipleship, and outreach.

At The Feast Ormoc, we believe that faith is not just something you carry privately — it is something you live out together. Our programs and ministries are designed to meet people wherever they are in their faith journey and invite them into a deeper encounter with God and authentic community. Whether you come for the weekly Feast or find a home in one of our ministries, you will be welcomed, challenged, and loved.

Flagship Gathering

The Feast

🙏

Our flagship weekly worship event — a powerful, Spirit-filled gathering that features uplifting praise and worship, a life-changing talk rooted in the Catholic faith, and a time of ministry and prayer. The Feast is the heart of everything we do.

What Happens at The Feast

Every Sunday is a journey — from worship to Word to community.

🎶

Praise & Worship

We begin with powerful praise and worship music that opens our hearts and draws us into God's presence. Songs of joy, faith, and surrender — sung together as one community.

🎙️

Talk / Homily

An inspiring, practical message rooted in Scripture and Catholic teaching — delivered in a way that is relevant to everyday life. You will leave with something real to apply during the week.

🕊️

Ministry Time

A time for prayer, healing, and encounter. Whether you need comfort, guidance, or simply a moment with God, our ministry team is ready to pray with and for you.

Find Your Place Here

There is a ministry for you, a community waiting to welcome you, and a purpose you were created to fulfill. Come find it with us.
